How To Play

Steps:

1. Choose a Story:

Find a Mad Libs story that you'd like to complete.

2. Fill in the Blanks:

Once you choose your story you will be prompted to fill in the blanks with certain types of words.

3. Read the Story Aloud:

Once all the blanks are filled, read the completed story out loud. Prepare for laughs, because the combinations of random words often result in hilarious and silly sentences!
